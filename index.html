Welcome to jambajabuc.github.io...

est. 2024

In sixth grade, on a computer my dad had let me use, I built up my first website on Weebly to advertise a gaming YouTube channel I had been putting all of my spare time and energy into. It had the bare essentials. My YouTube handle, my Instagram, mostly blank pages filled with small text lined with my hopes for the future. I quickly found out how difficult building, and then maintaining a website it. So, in the fashion of a middle schooler, my attention shifted to many other things I wanted to do.

A couple of years later I took an elective all about web development. What you see here is the bit I have retained since that time many, many years ago, and then some. Very suddenly, plastering the "Hello World" text on the top of a screen without direct instruction became the hardest thing I have ever done as an eighth grader.

Now, at twenty two. I write this small excerpt thinking about a simpler time, but a just as exciting one.

About Me

Jacob Hernandez is a 22-year-old multimedia artist based in Chicago. He began attending Columbia College Chicago in 2021 at the age of 18. In the free time he gets, he is usually spending it playing Destiny 2, watching a movie, or wandering about taking pictures of whatever he sees. He's been editing since middle school, honing his skills in Windows Movie Maker. Eventually moving on up to other software such as Sony Vegas, Premiere Pro, with a little bit of Final Cut and Avid thrown into the mix.
